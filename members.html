Co-President

Sui Feng Xu

I am Sui Feng. I am known as Mr.Canada because I spent about ten years of my life there where I discovered the activity of Quiz Bowl. After arriving in San Ramon at the begining of 9th grade, I was a little confused by the American system of education. Luckily, I found the right direction and was able to found Quiz Bowl my junior year. If you have any questions about the club or Quiz Bowl in general, I will be more than happy to explain it to you. For those who hesitate whether trivia is for you, I want to tell you my personal experience: "You do know more than you think!". The club is not about memorizing random facts but is about having fun noticing what you are capable of.
