History of the Club

The club was founded in 2013 in Dougherty Valley High School. In its first year of operation, the club was able to attract 23 active members and was able to pull 3 active Quiz Bowl teams. For the school year 2013-2014, it is expected that 4 teams will actively participate in tournaments around the Bay Area.
