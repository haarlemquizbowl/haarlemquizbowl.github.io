Middle School Quiz Bowl

In 2014, Haarlem Quiz Bowl decided to expand the Quiz Bowl experience to the middle schoolers. For the school year 2014-2015, Haarlem Quiz Bowl plans to work with Diablo Vista Middle School in Danville, Gale Ranch Middle School in San Ramon, Iron Horse Middle School in San Ramon and Windermere Ranch Middle School in San Ramon.


Diablo Vista Middle School

This school is already initiated in Quiz Bowl, dominating the Bay Area Middle School circuit with a first place finish in the University of California, Berkeley annual Middle school tournament. Yet, the school finished 54th at the 2014 Middle School National Championship Tournament at Minneapolis.

Gale Ranch Middle School

No need of explanations, this school finished 29th out of the 128 teams that attended the 2014 Middle School National Championship Tournament at Minneapolis. It also finished 3rd at the Stratford Middle School Invitational.

Windermere Ranch Middle School

We will introduce Quiz Bowl at this school as part of our Middle School Outreach program. We do feel there is an immense potential because the school ranked 4th last year in the state in terms of API scores.
