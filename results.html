2013-2014 Quiz Bowl Season results

Amador Valley Novice - 26 OctoberAt Haarlem Quiz Bowl's first ever tournament, Haarlem A finished 1st.

California Cup 1 - 9 November Haarlem A finished 5th of the top bracket in JV, Haarlem B finished 4th of the lower bracket in JV.

California Cup 2 - 23 NovemberHaarlem A finished 12th and Haarlem B finished 18th of the JV division.

California Cup 4 - 1 FebruaryHaarlem A finished 2nd of the Beta bracket in JV, Haarlem B finished 6th of the Beta bracket in JV.

Northern California State Championship - 15 March Haarlem A finished 2nd of the Larry bracket in Varsity and Haarlem B finished 3rd of the Moe bracket in Varsity.

California Cup 5 - 19 April Haarlem A finished 3rd, Haarlem B finished 12th, Haarlem C finished 25th in their first ever tournament.

High School National Championship Tournament - 31 MayHaarlem A finished 174th out of 272 teams in its first ever participation in a national-level tournament.
