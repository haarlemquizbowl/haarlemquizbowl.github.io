FAQ

Q: Where can I get those Quiz Bowl T-shirt that is recommended to wear at tournaments?

You can ask Bibhav Poudel the treasaurer via loopmail at the begining of the school year.

Q: Does it cost to be in Haarlem Quiz Bowl?

A: It does not cost anything to join and to attend the practices. However, we require the members to pay a determined amount for each tournament, as it required by the host, and we encourage you to pay a donation at the begining of the year to allow you to get discounts for each tournament.

Q: How many hours of involvement will I be required to put in Quiz Bowl?

A: We do not force any of our members to study but we recommend to attend the weekly meeting and to study about three hours per week. The effort that you put in your study will show up in tournaments. We have a perfect of Cody Zeng, the Co-President who only discovered Quiz Bowl last year, and became an important player for the team A after spending numerous hours studying.
